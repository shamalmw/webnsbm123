NSBM Green University Town-At the end the dream came true...


The NSBM Green University Town is scheduled to be declared open by His Excellency the president Maithripala Sirisena, along with Hon Prime Minister Ranil Wickremasinghe, by the invitation of Minister of Skills Development and Vocational Training, Hon Mahinda Samarasinghe and State Minister of Skills Development and Vocational Training, Hon Palitha Range Bandara. This state-of-the-art modern university is the first and the only of it's kind in the whole of South Asia and will hold 30,000 undergraduates at a given time.

President's Visit to NSBM Green University Town...


NSBM Green University Town, the first and the only one of it's kind in the whole of South Asia is to be inaugurated very soon. Today HE The President paid a visit to the facility and there was a tree plantation done as a remembrance of the event as well. HE The President voiced his ideas about the venture, and was of the opinion that this is an ideal gateway for the Sri Lankan youth to shine, within the country as well as internationally.

NSBM Swimming Team became the overall Champions...


NSBM swimming team became the overall champions of Flip Turn '16, Inter university swimming meet organised by SAITM by securing the first place in 17 individual events, second place in 7 events and 3rd place in 1 event. It was held on 12th June 2016 at Thurstan swimming pool complex.
Team members are - Pathum Edirisinghe, Shan Dias, Kanitha Munasinghe, Methma Jayawardena, Hasanthi Nugawela, Sanara De Silva, Keshan Munasinghe, Sachil Perera and Jehani Goonathilaka

Workshop on Introduction to Electronics and Arduino...


A free workshop was held on 2nd July at School of Computing, NSBM, to learn about basic electronics and introduction to Arduino from 9.30 am to 2.00 pm. The sessions were based on “How to plan a successful electronics project” and “Basic electronics and Introduction to Arduino”
